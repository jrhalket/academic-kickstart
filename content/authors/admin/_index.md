Jonathan Halket is an Assistant Professor and the Helen and O.N. Mitchell, Jr Faculty Fellow in Real Estate at the Mays Business School, Texas A&M University and a Research Fellow at the Institute for Fiscal Studies and the Centre for Microdata Methods and Practice in London, UK. He researches the intersection of finance, the built environment, urban spaces and macroeconomics.
